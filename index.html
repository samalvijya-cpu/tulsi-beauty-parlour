About Our Beauty Services

Cleanup – Benefits

Cleanup is a basic skin care treatment that helps remove dirt, excess oil, and dead skin cells from the face. Regular cleanup keeps the skin fresh, improves blood circulation, reduces pimples and blackheads, and gives a natural glow. It is perfect for maintaining healthy skin even if you don’t have time for a full facial.

Facial – Benefits

Facial treatments deeply nourish the skin and help improve skin texture and tone. Facials reduce tanning, pigmentation, and signs of tiredness while making the skin soft, smooth, and glowing. At Tulsi Beauty Parlour, we choose facials according to your skin type for best and safe results.

Makeup Services

Our professional makeup services are suitable for weddings, parties, and special occasions. We focus on enhancing your natural beauty using quality products that suit your skin tone. Bridal and party makeup is done with proper skin preparation for a long-lasting and elegant look.

Haircut & Styling

A good haircut can completely change your look and boost confidence. We offer simple to stylish haircuts along with basic hair styling that suits your face shape and lifestyle. Hygiene and comfort are always maintained during every service.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which facial is best for my skin?

The best facial depends on your skin type and concerns. Our beautician examines your skin and suggests the most suitable facial for safe and effective results.

How often should I do facial or cleanup?

Cleanup can be done once a month to maintain skin health. Facials are recommended every 4–6 weeks depending on your skin condition.

Are the products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, we use quality and skin-friendly products. For sensitive skin, services are done carefully after proper consultation.
